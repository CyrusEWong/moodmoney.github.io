Mood Money

Background and Creation

This project was brainstormed and the notion was developed at the first Financial Planning Academy at Cal State Fullerton. This is an expansion of said idea.

Mood Money

Most budgeting apps fail because they treat money as pure math. Ignoring the emotional side of spending. People don’t overspend because they’re bad at numbers, they do it because of stress, boredom, or social pressure. Traditional apps shame users with red alerts and rigid categories, which only fuels guilt and abandonment. Worse, they assume everyone has predictable incomes and willpower, leaving freelancers, ADHDers, and emotional spenders behind.
